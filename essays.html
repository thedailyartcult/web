I built The Daily Art Cult because I believe most people know more about art than they think — they've simply never been given the chance to prove it to themselves. The guessing game is the entry point. These essays are what I actually think.

I write about painting the way philosophers write about anything they love but cannot fully explain. With obsession, uncertainty, and the quiet hope that writing it down might bring me closer to understanding it.

I am not an academic. I am someone who has spent too many hours in too many museums, arguing with the paintings, and occasionally losing.
